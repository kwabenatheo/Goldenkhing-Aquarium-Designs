About Goldenkhing Aquarium Designs

Goldenkhing Aquarium Designs is a leading aquarium design company dedicated to transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary aquatic environments. With over a decade of experience, we specialize in custom aquariums for residential, commercial, and corporate clients.

Our team consists of passionate marine experts, designers, and engineers who work closely with you from concept to installation and beyond. Whether you want a stunning reef tank, a serene freshwater setup, or a showstopping centerpiece, we bring your vision to life with elegance and precision.

We pride ourselves on our craftsmanship, innovation, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Meet Our CEO

Daniel Kunansi Benson, the visionary behind Goldenkhing Aquarium Designs, brings over four years of expertise in aquarium building and maintenance services. Specializing in aqua servicing, custom aquarium builds, aqua ponds, makeovers, and decorative designs, Daniel is passionate about delivering exceptional results and ensuring client satisfaction through quick and reliable service.

In his previous role at Water World Company, Daniel was responsible for maintenance management, where he honed his skills in creating and maintaining stunning aquatic environments. Based in Kasoa, he is dedicated to continuous learning and innovation, always striving to elevate the art of aquarium design. Daniel looks forward to connecting with like-minded individuals and exploring new opportunities to transform spaces with breathtaking aquatic creations.
