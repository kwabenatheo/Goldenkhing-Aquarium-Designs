Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my aquarium?

Every 2–4 weeks depending on the setup. We offer maintenance plans to make it easier!

Do you offer custom shapes and sizes?

Absolutely! We design aquariums that fit any space – from small cubes to large wall units.

What types of aquariums do you build?

We design freshwater, saltwater, reef tanks, and more, tailored to client needs.

Do you provide fish and aquatic plants too?

Yes. We source and recommend suitable fish, corals, and plants based on your tank type.
